Access Information: Including your access time, duration of visit, page paths visited, content viewed, click records, IP address (which may be anonymized after processing by Cloudflare), internet service provider (ISP), and geographic region. 3. Technical Log Information: Including server response times, error logs, access request headers, and other technical data.

This Website uses Cloudflare CDN services for website acceleration and security protection. Cloudflare may collect information related to your visit, including but not limited to IP addresses, browser types, access times, and page requests for node scheduling, attack prevention, and access statistics. Cloudflare's data processing follows its own privacy policy (please refer to the Cloudflare official privacy policy page). The advertisements displayed on this Website are non-data-collecting advertisements. No personal information (including but not limited to browsing behavior, interests, device identifiers, or location information) is collected during the ad delivery process. Only when you voluntarily choose to participate in specific interactive features or services may we collect relevant information you provide, such as: 1. If you choose to share opinions or ask questions through third-party embedded features (such as third-party comment plugins), the relevant information will be collected and processed by the corresponding third-party service provider according to their privacy policy. This Website does not store such information. 2. If you participate in resource sharing or technical exchange activities organized by this Website, we may collect necessary information (such as verification info for resource access) as required by the activity rules. Such information will only be used for activity-related purposes and will be deleted or anonymized after the activity concludes.

(1) Use by This Website

The non-personally identifiable information we collect is used only for the following legitimate purposes: 1. Providing and Maintaining Services: Ensuring the Website operates normally and, in conjunction with Cloudflare CDN, optimizing loading speeds and stability to provide a smooth browsing experience. 2. Content Optimization: Analyzing overall user preferences based on access data (such as page views and click heatmaps) to optimize layout, update frequency, and provide technical blogs and resources that better meet user needs. 3. Security Protection: Combining Cloudflare's security capabilities to analyze access logs, identify and prevent malicious attacks, network fraud, and other security risks to protect your and the Website's rights. 4. Compliance: Complying with relevant laws, regulations, and regulatory requirements, and cooperating with the lawful investigations of authorized authorities.

Use of collected information by Cloudflare: Primarily for providing CDN acceleration, DDoS protection, and routing optimization. Details are subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

(1) Storage and Protection by This Website

Because this Website is a static site without a database, it does not store any personal information (including both voluntarily provided and automatically collected info). All temporary access data collected automatically is used only for real-time analysis and security verification and will not be retained.

(2) Storage and Protection by Third-Party Services

Access information collected by Cloudflare is stored on its secure servers according to its own data storage policies. Cloudflare employs industry-standard security measures (such as data encryption and access controls). The storage period is subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

(3) Joint Security Responsibility

We have carefully selected a third-party service provider (Cloudflare) with high privacy protection standards and require them to comply with relevant laws and take necessary security measures. However, due to the uncertainties of the internet, we cannot fully guarantee the absolute security of information transmitted or stored by third-party providers. You should take care to protect your own device information and network environment.

V. Information Sharing and Disclosure

(1) Sharing and Disclosure by This Website

This Website does not store any personal information and does not collect data via ads; therefore, we do not share, sell, or rent personal information to third parties. We may only disclose relevant non-personally identifiable information (such as aggregated statistics) in the following circumstances: 1. With your explicit consent; 2. As required by laws, regulations, judicial rulings, or mandatory administrative orders; 3. To protect the legitimate rights of this Website, you, or other third parties within a reasonable and necessary scope.

(2) Sharing and Disclosure by Third-Party Services

Cloudflare's sharing and disclosure of information follow its own privacy policy and relevant laws.

VI. Use of Cookies and Similar Tracking Technologies

(1) Cookie Usage by This Website

This Website does not actively set its own Cookies and does not set any tracking Cookies for advertising functions.

(2) Cookie Usage by Third-Party Services

Disabling Cookies may affect the loading speed or functionality of certain third-party services, but it will not affect ad display or core browsing functions.
